Awards

  • Best book on software design: A Philosophy Of Software Design. This is probably the best book I‚Äôve ever read on software design.

  • Worst classic: How to Win Friends & Influence People. How about ‚ÄúHow to be a psychopath?‚Äù Actually, the very first bit on morals was pretty good. But the rest of the book was all manipulation and gross.

  • Best book about gender/sex: Born Both: An Intersex Life. A great book about how sex is often as arbitrary as gender.

  • Best YA Feminist Fantasy book: Tess of the Road: A young heroine takes to road to slay a dragon and the dragon is internalized mysogony.

  • Best RPG: Troika! Troika really captured my imagination this year.
